The Drawer

What is the drawer?

  • You can store pieces in your drawer and they won't show on the field.
  • This is nice if you want to avoid having to remove huge stacks of pieces.

Good to know

  • The drawer only works for pieces that have never been moved manually.
  • The actions of the drawer are purely local. Others cannot see them, and on refresh it resets too.

How does it work?

  • The drawer shows the number of pieces that you have withdrawn.
  • You can select how many pieces you want to withdraw or deposit.
  • Move to drawer: remove pieces from the board to the drawer.
  • Move from drawer: move pieces back to the board in the area you specified.
